The Wisconsin Men's Roller Derby team are made up of 2 home teams from all over this Goodland, specifically the Milwaukee, Madison and Appleton areas. We play our home team games in Wisconsin and travel all over the USA battling other teams in the Men's Roller Derby Association (MRDA). Our home teams compete January through April to take home The Golden Skate. After the home season, the MRDA season kicks off and the best of our skaters travel all over the USA competing against other teams with aspirations of making it to MRDA Champs in October. Follow us on Facebook , Twitter , or Instagram to see where we're headed next! If this sounds like something you'd like to get into, email us at info@mensderbyWI.com and/or join our recruits Facebook group WMRD Recruits .
